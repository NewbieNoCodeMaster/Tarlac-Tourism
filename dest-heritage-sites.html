Heritage Sites

Tarlac has deep roots in Philippine history. The province was home to national heroes and pivotal moments in the country's fight for democracy. Its heritage sites preserve these stories for generations to come.

  • Aquino Center & Museum
  • Capas National Shrine
  • Tarlac Provincial Capitol
  • Aquino Center & Museum: A tribute to Ninoy and Cory Aquino in Tarlac City, housing personal memorabilia, photographs, and artifacts that chronicle their lives and legacy.
  • Capas National Shrine: A solemn memorial dedicated to Filipino and American soldiers who perished during the Bataan Death March in World War II.
  • Tarlac Provincial Capitol: An architectural landmark at the heart of Tarlac City that has stood as the seat of provincial governance for over a century.
