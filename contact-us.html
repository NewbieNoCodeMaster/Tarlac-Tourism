The Tarlac Tourism Office is dedicated to showcasing the best of Tarlac Province – from its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural attractions to its delicious cuisine and warm community spirit. Below you’ll find details about our work, plus a form to send us your messages, questions, or feedback.

Our Mission

  • To promote Tarlac as a premier travel destination in Central Luzon by highlighting its unique cultural, historical, and natural attractions.
  • To support local communities by developing sustainable tourism initiatives that create jobs and boost the provincial economy.
  • To preserve and protect Tarlac’s cultural heritage and natural resources for future generations.
  • To provide exceptional service and information to visitors, ensuring their stay in Tarlac is safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

Our Vision

We envision Tarlac as a globally recognized destination where responsible tourism thrives, local communities prosper, and the province’s natural beauty and cultural identity are celebrated and preserved. We aim to make Tarlac a place where visitors feel welcome, inspired, and connected to the heart of Luzon.

Core Goals

  • Boost Visitor Numbers
  • Empower Local People
  • Preserve Natural Spaces
  • Goal 1: Increase Tourism Awareness – Launch targeted marketing campaigns to showcase Tarlac’s attractions to both domestic and international travelers.
  • Goal 2: Enhance Tourism Infrastructure – Work with local governments to improve roads, signage, rest areas, and visitor facilities across the province.
  • Goal 3: Support Local Businesses – Partner with accommodations, restaurants, and craft makers to help them reach more visitors and grow sustainably.
  • Goal 4: Protect Natural & Cultural Heritage – Implement programs to conserve historical sites, wildlife habitats, and traditional practices.
  • Goal 5: Improve Visitor Experience – Develop comprehensive travel guides, train tour guides, and set up visitor information centers in key locations.

Core Values

  • Integrity – We operate with honesty and transparency in all our work.
  • Community First – We prioritize the well-being and prosperity of Tarlac’s local communities.
  • Sustainability – We ensure all tourism activities protect the environment and preserve cultural heritage.
  • Excellence – We strive to provide the highest quality service and experiences for visitors.
  • Inclusivity – We welcome travelers from all backgrounds and ensure tourism benefits everyone in Tarlac.
